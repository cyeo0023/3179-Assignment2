Impact of Coronavirus on the world

In 2020, are you aware of how many confirmed cases of Coronavirus there are in the world?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Coronavirus have spread to many countries, this visualisation allows us to observe the total confirmed cases of Coronavirus that occured in the world.


Countries have conducted Coronavirus tests on people to avoid people spreading the virus

This bubble plot shows the number of tests conducted in the month of May 2020. The tests are separated into two categories by the countries, which are samples and cases tests.


To counter Coronavirus, vaccinations are created.

Vaccinations are done by countries to increase the immunity of people to control the severe symptoms Coronavirus brings.
